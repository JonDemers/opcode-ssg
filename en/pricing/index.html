Flexible Engagement Models

Every project is different. We offer two engagement models to match your needs: ongoing collaboration billed hourly, or fixed-price delivery for well-defined projects. Both start with a free consultation to understand your requirements and determine the best approach.


Hourly Consulting

Best for:

  • Ongoing projects with evolving requirements
  • Enterprise clients with complex systems
  • Projects requiring flexibility and iteration
  • Long-term technical partnerships

How it works:

After our initial consultation, we provide a proposal outlining our hourly rate and availability. You get regular progress updates and can adjust priorities as needed. Billed for actual time worked.

Fixed-Price Delivery

Best for:

  • Small businesses and startups
  • Projects with clear, written specifications
  • Well-defined scope that we can deliver independently
  • Clients who prefer predictable budgets

Requirements:

Detailed written specs, timely collaboration, and defined scope. Includes 2-4 milestone demos for feedback. Scope changes incur additional fees, communicated upfront.


What You Get

Expert execution – We make things work and fix what's broken

Full-stack capabilities – Web applications, APIs, cloud infrastructure, consulting

AI-enhanced productivity – Leveraging modern tools for faster delivery

Complete source code – With documentation (README.md)

Flexible deployment – Your cloud or ours

Optional support & maintenance – Available at additional cost


Getting Started

  1. Free 30-45 minute video consultation (Teams, Zoom, or Google Meet) to discuss your business model, project needs, and technical requirements
  2. Follow-up via email with any additional details or questions
  3. Detailed proposal outlining how we can help, pricing, timeline, and engagement terms
  4. Contract and kickoff once you approve the proposal

No obligation. No pressure. Just a conversation to see if we're the right fit for your project.
